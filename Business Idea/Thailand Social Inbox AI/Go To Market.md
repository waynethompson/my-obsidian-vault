## Positioning

Position it as a revenue and responsiveness product, not as a generic chatbot.

Core message:

> We help Thai businesses convert more Facebook and LINE conversations into bookings, leads, and sales without hiring more admin staff.

## Best first niches

Prioritize businesses where:

- customers already message before buying
- response speed matters
- the average customer value is meaningful
- staff waste time on repetitive questions

Best candidates:

- clinics
- property
- premium home services
- relocation and visa services
- private education
- travel planning

## Commercial wedge

Sell this in two layers:

1. Done-for-you setup and playbook design
2. Monthly software and optimization fee

That keeps the first sale easier while still building toward a product business.

## Why this should sell

- the problem is visible in a live inbox
- businesses already know they are slow to reply
- Thailand-specific channel behavior makes the wedge sharper
- the ROI can be framed in bookings, quotes, and saved staff time

## Sales motion

Use the existing ideas as the acquisition engine:

1. Use [[Business Idea/Infinite Brain/AI Lead Gen/AI Lead Response and Booking Automation|AI Lead Gen]] style audits to show missed inbox leads.
2. Offer a short pilot on one Facebook Page or LINE account.
3. Use the pilot results to sell recurring software and optimization.
4. Expand the account into CRM, outbound, and pipeline workflows through the [[Business Idea/Infinite Brain/Sales Tool/Product Features and Phases|Sales Tool]] vision.

## Offer structure

Example structure:

- setup fee for onboarding, playbooks, and channel connection
- monthly platform fee by inbox or business location
- optional premium support for campaign changes and playbook tuning

## Key proof points to collect early

- first response time before vs after
- number of qualified leads captured
- bookings created from chat
- staff time saved on repetitive replies
- percentage of conversations escalated to humans

## Long-term value

If this works, it can become a strong local-market platform because it sits where Thai customer demand actually appears. It also creates a natural bridge between inbound chat conversion and the broader sales operating system in the Sales Tool idea.
