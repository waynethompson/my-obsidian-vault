# Thailand Social Inbox AI — User Personas

## Overview

Thailand Social Inbox AI is an AI inbox copilot for Thai businesses managing customer conversations through Facebook Page inbox and LINE Official Account. The product targets businesses where a single conversation can turn into significant revenue and slow responses cost real money.

---

## Persona 1 — The Aesthetic Clinic Owner / Manager

**Who they are**
Owner or manager of an aesthetic clinic (botox, filler, skin treatments) with 3–20 staff. Based in Bangkok or a major Thai city. Running Facebook ads that drive inquiries into the Page inbox. Gets 50–150 messages per day. Staff reply when they can, often hours later.

**What they want**
- Every incoming message gets an instant, Thai-language reply
- AI qualifies the lead (what treatment, budget, preferred date) before staff engage
- Booking details captured in a structured format
- Staff notified only when a lead is warm and ready to book
- Clear dashboard: how many inquiries, how many became bookings

**Language requirement:** Must communicate naturally in Thai. Switching to English where appropriate (e.g., international patients). Tone must feel warm and professional — not robotic.

**Key frustrations**
- Leads that came in at 10pm are cold by the time staff reply at 9am
- Staff copy-paste the same responses all day — inconsistent quality
- No way to see which Facebook ad campaign is generating the most bookings
- Patient details collected in chat are re-entered into a separate booking system by hand

**Success looks like**
Response time drops from hours to under 30 seconds. Staff only deal with confirmed booking requests. The owner sees a clean report of weekly inquiry volume and conversion rate.

## Persona 2 — The Property Agent / Real Estate Agency

**Who they are**
A real estate agent or small property agency handling condo sales, rentals, or commercial listings in Thailand. Age 30–55. Generates leads through Facebook ads, property portals, and organic posts. LINE OA is their primary channel for ongoing lead nurturing.

**What they want**
- AI qualifies leads immediately: budget range, location preference, timeline, buy or rent
- Follow-up messages sent automatically to leads who have not responded in 24 hours
- Hot leads escalated to the agent instantly via notification
- Conversation history and lead details visible in a simple CRM view

**Key frustrations**
- Hundreds of messages arrive after a new listing is posted — impossible to triage manually
- Agents forget to follow up with leads who said "maybe next month"
- High-value leads get lost among tyre-kickers
- No consistent qualification process across the team

**Success looks like**
Every inquiry is triaged and qualified within minutes. Agents spend their day on calls with pre-qualified leads, not reading through unqualified messages.

## Persona 3 — The Tour Operator / Travel Agent

**Who they are**
A Thai travel agency or tour operator selling packages to domestic and international tourists. Receives booking inquiries via Facebook Messenger and LINE. Age 28–50. Seasonal demand peaks (Songkran, Chinese New Year) overwhelm the inbox.

**What they want**
- AI handles common questions: itinerary, price, availability, group size
- Quote details captured: number of travellers, dates, specific requests
- Leads handed off to a human only for final confirmation and payment
- Multilingual capability: Thai, English, Chinese (for Chinese tourist market)

**Key frustrations**
- Peak season inquiry volume overwhelms one or two staff
- International tourists ask in English, Mandarin, or Korean — staff struggle to reply quickly
- Same itinerary questions answered dozens of times daily
- Inquiries lost when a staff member is sick or on holiday

**Success looks like**
AI handles 70–80% of inquiry volume fully autonomously. Human staff focus on closing confirmed bookings. Seasonal peaks are handled without hiring extra staff.

---

## Persona 4 — The Home Services Business Owner

**Who they are**
Owner of a home services business — cleaning, aircon servicing, pest control, renovation — marketed primarily through Facebook. Age 30–55. Replies to quotes via Messenger. Business is growing but inbox management is becoming a bottleneck.

**What they want**
- Instant reply to quote requests with basic pricing or a structured quote form
- Service area, job type, and preferred date captured automatically
- Confirmation messages sent once a booking is agreed
- Simple weekly summary: how many jobs booked vs enquiries received

**Key frustrations**
- Takes too long to get back to customers — they book elsewhere in the meantime
- Staff answering messages are not empowered to confirm pricing without checking
- No record of how many quotes were sent vs how many became jobs
- Customers sometimes ghost after receiving a quote — no follow-up system

**Success looks like**
Every quote inquiry gets an instant reply with clear pricing guidance. Confirmed bookings are logged. A simple weekly report shows quote volume and conversion rate.
