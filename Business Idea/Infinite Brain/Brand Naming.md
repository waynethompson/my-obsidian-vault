## Current naming direction

**Strong direction:** use **InfiniteBrain** as the company or umbrella brand, and use **Layer** names for products.

That gives a clean brand architecture:

- **InfiniteBrain** — parent brand
- **LeadLayer** — sales
- **SupportLayer** — support
- **HiringLayer** — recruiting
- **OpsLayer** — operations
- **GrowthLayer** — marketing/growth

## Why it works

- **InfiniteBrain** feels AI-native, expansive, and premium
- **Layer** feels lightweight, modular, and product-suite friendly
- It leaves room to position each tool as "an AI layer for X"

## Naming structures

1. **InfiniteBrain LeadLayer** — most complete, company + product
2. **LeadLayer by InfiniteBrain** — strongest for product-first branding
3. **LeadLayer AI** — simpler if you want the product to stand alone
4. **The Layer Suite** — good umbrella name for the product family

## Best current option

**LeadLayer by InfiniteBrain**

That sounds startup-y, flexible, and scalable in a way **infinitebrain.ai** alone does not.
