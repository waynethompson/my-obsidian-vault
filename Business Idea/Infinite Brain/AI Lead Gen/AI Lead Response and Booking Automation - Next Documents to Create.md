## Definition of done for this document set

The supporting set is strong enough when:

- you have a short asset to send before or after a sales call,
- you have a repeatable audit that creates urgency,
- you can show simple ROI math for the economic buyer,
- you can deliver pilots in a consistent way,
- you can turn results into proof,
- and you can tailor the pitch by niche.

## Notes

- The first two documents are the most useful if the immediate goal is to start selling.
- The ROI calculator and case study template directly support the "use the product to sell the product" strategy.
- The niche playbook can start as one niche only, not a full library.
