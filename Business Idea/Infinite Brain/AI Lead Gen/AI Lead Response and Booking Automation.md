This is the best fit for the fastest cash in 30 days.

You sell a fixed-price productized service that helps high-value service businesses stop losing inbound leads by automating:

- instant lead response
- qualification
- follow-up sequences
- booking reminders
- missed inquiry recovery
- simple admin dashboard or reporting

## Best buyers

Target businesses where one extra customer is worth a lot:

- aesthetic clinics
- dental clinics
- property agents or agencies
- visa or relocation agencies
- legal or accounting firms
- tour operators selling premium packages

This is a strong fit in Thailand because many businesses handle leads poorly across website forms, Facebook, LINE, WhatsApp, and email.

## Why this can sell within 48 hours

- the pain is obvious and expensive
- owners already understand that missed leads equal lost money
- you can pitch revenue improvement, not just AI
- you can show a simple mockup or process map on the first call
- your wife can help localize and sell into Thai businesses

## Offer structure

**Core offer:** "We install a lead response and follow-up system that contacts new inquiries immediately, qualifies them, and pushes qualified leads into your booking or sales process."

This should be framed as a **productized service**, not a SaaS platform: fixed scope, fast implementation, clear business outcome, and optional ongoing support.

## Pricing

- setup fee: **$1,500 to $4,000**
- optional monthly support: **$300 to $1,000**

With only 2 to 3 clients in 30 days, this can materially outperform most small product ideas.

## Delivery model

Use existing tools and lightweight custom glue code instead of building a platform:

- make.com or n8n
- Twilio, email, and LINE-compatible workflow where appropriate
- lightweight custom dashboard if needed
- AI-assisted message drafting and qualification logic

## 48-hour sales plan

1. Pick one niche first, ideally aesthetic clinics or property agencies.
2. Build a one-page pitch and one short demo flow.
3. Make a list of 50 prospects in Thailand and 50 English-speaking prospects online.
4. Offer a **free missed-lead audit** and show how faster response increases bookings.
5. Close with a fixed-price "done in 5 days" implementation.

## Why this is a good fit for you

- strong match with automation, internal tools, and app-building skills
- high-value business outcome
- easy to explain on calls
- wife adds local language advantage
- low capital requirement

## Positioning

Frame this as a business outcome:

- faster response to inquiries
- fewer dropped leads
- more booked calls or appointments
- better visibility into where leads come from and what happens next

## Use the product to sell the product (Self-sell strategy)

Treat the productized service as both the solution and the sales engine. Use live missed-lead audits, demo installs, and pilots to generate and convert prospects:

- Run free "missed-lead audits" using the product to show concrete missed opportunities and a quick ROI estimate.
- Deploy a lightweight demo or pilot for local prospects and use the pilot's uplift as proof in pitches.
- Capture testimonial snippets, screenshots, and conversion metrics from early clients and use them in outreach templates.
- Automate referral and testimonial requests after successful bookings to create a growth loop.
- Use the admin dashboard and live metrics as a trust signal during sales calls.
