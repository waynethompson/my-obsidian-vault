## Portfolio direction

The current shape suggests a cleaner strategy than before:

1. Use **AI Lead Response and Booking Automation** to generate revenue and learn the workflow.
2. Build **Infinite Brain** as the longer-term product suite around lead generation, sales workflow, and CRM control.
3. Use **Thailand Social Inbox AI** as either a strong standalone Thailand wedge or a future inbound/support layer in the broader suite.
4. Keep everything else on a strict review or parked list until one primary bet is clearly working.
