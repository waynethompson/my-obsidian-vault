# 5-Day Custom Internal Tool Sprint

Related notes:

- [[Business Idea/Fast Money Making Ideas/Fast_Money_Making_Ideas]]

## Summary

This is the best second offer for larger single deals.

You sell a fixed-scope sprint where you replace one painful manual workflow with a working internal tool or dashboard in 5 business days.

## Example deliverables

- lead tracking dashboard
- quotation generator
- inventory or order tracker
- staff task and approval workflow
- customer follow-up dashboard
- reporting portal for management

## Best buyers

Focus on businesses already running on messy spreadsheets, chat apps, and manual copy-paste:

- small agencies
- recruiters
- import/export businesses
- travel businesses
- service companies with admin-heavy workflows
- Thai SMEs with growing teams

## Why this can sell within 48 hours

- buyers already know the pain
- the pitch is simple: save time, reduce mistakes, improve visibility
- you can sell a fixed outcome instead of an open-ended dev project
- strong LinkedIn and work history provide credibility even without a polished public profile

## Offer structure

**Core offer:** "In 5 days, we replace one spreadsheet-heavy workflow with a simple internal tool your team can actually use."

## Pricing

- fixed sprint: **$2,500 to $7,500**
- follow-on support or enhancements: **$500 to $2,000**

Even one good client can make the month.

## Delivery model

Keep scope narrow and reusable:

- authentication
- role-based screens only if necessary
- one workflow
- one dashboard
- one export or report function

Use the stack that lets you move fastest with low risk and simple hosting.

## 48-hour sales plan

1. Offer a **free workflow teardown** for one business process.
2. Ask the owner to screen-share the spreadsheet or process.
3. Diagnose time loss, errors, and bottlenecks live on the call.
4. Propose a fixed-price sprint with a clear before and after outcome.
5. Ask for 50 percent upfront to reserve the slot.

## Why this is a good fit for you

- leverages your engineering depth directly
- MBA helps frame ROI and process improvement
- higher contract values than most freelance coding gigs
- no audience required, only targeted outreach
