
Here’s the fun part — you’re essentially designing the digital backbone for Australia’s community groups, clubs, and scouting organisations. That’s a huge market with very fragmented tools today, so a well‑designed platform could become the default home for grassroots organisations.

Below is a structured ideation of features, grouped into logical layers so you can see how the product could evolve from MVP to ecosystem.

---

# 🌱 **Core Platform Features (MVP)**

These are the essentials that let a club create a presence, manage members, and run basic operations.

## **1. Group Website Builder**

- Customisable templates for clubs, scouts, sports teams, hobby groups
- Drag‑and‑drop sections: About, Join Us, Events, News, Gallery
- Custom domain support
- Mobile‑friendly by default
- Accessibility‑compliant (WCAG)

## **2. Membership & Joining**

- Online membership forms
- Custom fields (e.g., Working With Children Check, emergency contacts)
- Automated approval workflows
- Membership tiers (junior, adult, family, volunteer)
- Renewal reminders and auto‑renew options
- Digital membership cards

## **3. Payments & Fundraising**

- Integrated payments (Stripe, PayPal, Apple/Google Pay)
- Sell memberships, events, merchandise
- Donation module with optional tax‑deductible receipts
- Fundraising campaigns with progress bars
- “Support our club” micro‑donations at checkout

## **4. Events & Ticketing**

- Event creation with capacity limits
- Free or paid tickets
- QR code check‑in
- Volunteer sign‑up slots
- Multi‑session events (e.g., weekly meetings, camps)

## **5. Merchandise Store**

- Simple e‑commerce for shirts, badges, gear
- Inventory tracking
- Pre‑order campaigns
- Print‑on‑demand integration (future)

---

# 🧭 **Community & Engagement Features**

These help clubs stay active and connected.

## **6. Communication Tools**

- Email newsletters
- SMS alerts (optional paid add‑on)
- In‑platform announcements
- Scheduled posts

## **7. Member Portal**

- Members can update details
- Access event history, tickets, receipts
- View volunteer hours
- Download documents (policies, forms)

## **8. Photo & Media Library**

- Secure gallery for events
- Permission controls for youth photos
- Tagging by event or year

---

# 🛠️ **Administration & Compliance**

Especially important for scouting and youth organisations.

## **9. Roles & Permissions**

- Admin, Treasurer, Event Manager, Communications Officer
- Custom role creation

## **10. Compliance Tools**

- Working With Children Check tracking
- Document storage (risk assessments, policies)
- Incident reporting
- Attendance logs

## **11. Financial Tools**

- Basic financial dashboard
- Export to Xero / MYOB
- Reconciliation of payments
- Automated receipts

## **13. Volunteer Management**

- Volunteer profiles
- Skill tagging

## **14. Learning & Badges**

For scouting‑style organisations:

# 🏪 **Marketplace & Ecosystem (Your future company portal idea)**

This is where things get exciting — you’re building a two‑sided marketplace.

## **15. Supplier Portal (B2B)**

Companies can:

- List products (uniforms, equipment, fundraising items)
- Offer special pricing for clubs
- Provide affiliate commissions to groups
- Run seasonal campaigns (e.g., “Back to Scouts”)

## **16. Community Group Marketplace**

Groups can:

- Add supplier products to their store
- Earn a margin on each sale
- Run fundraising drives with pre‑approved products
- Access bulk‑buy discounts

## **17. Revenue Sharing & Commission Engine**

- Commission rules per supplier
- Automated payouts
- Transparent reporting

- Built specifically for community groups (not generic website builders)
- Compliance tools for youth organisations (huge pain point)

- Multi‑branch support for national bodies
