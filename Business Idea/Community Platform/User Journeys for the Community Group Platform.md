🧑‍💼 **1. Admin Journey (Club Leader / Committee Member)**

**Goal:**

Set up and manage the group’s online presence, memberships, events, and fundraising.

**Journey Steps**

**1. Onboarding**

- Receives invitation or signs up as a new group admin
- Chooses a template for the group site
- Completes guided setup (branding, logo, colours, About page)

**2. Configure Membership**

- Creates membership tiers (Adult, Junior, Family)
- Customises membership form fields
- Sets pricing and renewal rules
- Enables digital membership cards

**3. Publish Group Website**

- Adds pages: Join Us, Events, News, Gallery
- Connects custom domain (optional)
- Publishes site publicly

**4. Manage Members**

- Reviews new membership applications
- Approves or declines
- Sends welcome emails
- Tracks renewals and overdue members

**5. Create Events & Fundraisers**

- Creates an event with date, location, capacity
- Sets ticket types (free/paid)
- Enables QR check‑in
- Shares event link with members

**6. Manage Store & Merchandise**

- Adds products (shirts, badges, gear)
- Sets inventory and pricing
- Processes orders and pickups

**7. Communications**

- Sends newsletters
- Posts announcements
- Schedules reminders for events or renewals

**8. Compliance & Admin**

- Uploads policies and risk assessments
- Tracks Working With Children Checks
- Reviews attendance logs

**9. Financial Oversight**

- Views payment dashboard
- Exports transactions to Xero/MYOB
- Reviews fundraising totals

👨‍👩‍👧 **2. Parent / Member Journey**

**Goal:**

Join a group, stay informed, register for events, and support fundraising.

**Journey Steps**

**1. Discover & Join**

- Finds group via directory or direct link
- Reads About page and membership options
- Completes online membership form
- Pays membership fee
- Receives digital membership card

**2. Access Member Portal**

- Logs in to view profile
- Updates contact details
- Views membership status and renewal date
- Downloads receipts

**3. Register for Events**

- Browses upcoming events
- Selects ticket type
- Pays (if required)
- Receives QR code ticket

**4. Support Fundraising**

- Buys merchandise
- Makes donations
- Participates in fundraising campaigns

**5. Stay Connected**

- Receives newsletters
- Reads announcements
- Views event photos (with permissions)

🧑‍🔧 **3. Volunteer Journey**

**Goal:**

Sign up for volunteer roles, track hours, and support group activities.

**Journey Steps**

**1. Join as Member or Volunteer**

- Signs up as a member or volunteer-only profile
- Provides relevant details (skills, WWCC number)

**2. View Volunteer Opportunities**

- Sees available roles for events (BBQ helper, first aid, setup crew)
- Filters by date, skill, or time commitment

**3. Sign Up for a Role**

- Selects a shift or task
- Receives confirmation email
- Adds to personal calendar

**4. Event Day**

- Checks in via QR code
- Completes assigned tasks

**5. Track Hours**

- Hours auto‑logged or manually submitted
- Views volunteer history in portal

**6. Recognition**

- Receives badges or certificates (Phase 2)
- Appears on volunteer leaderboard (optional)

🏢 **4. Supplier Journey (Phase 2 Marketplace)**

**Goal:**

List products for community groups to sell, manage orders, and track commissions.

**Journey Steps**

**1. Supplier Onboarding**

- Creates supplier account
- Provides business details and verification
- Sets commission rules or wholesale pricing

**2. Product Listing**

- Adds products (uniforms, equipment, fundraising items)
- Uploads images, descriptions, pricing
- Sets availability (seasonal, year‑round)

**3. Marketplace Visibility**

- Products appear in the community group marketplace
- Groups add items to their store with one click

**4. Order Flow**

- Group sells product to members
- Supplier receives order notification
- Ships product or provides digital delivery
- Updates order status

**5. Financials**

- Commission engine calculates payouts
- Supplier views sales dashboard
- Downloads reports

**6. Campaigns**

- Creates seasonal promotions (e.g., “Back to Scouts”)
- Offers bulk discounts
- Sends promotional materials to groups
