# **Community Group Platform Roadmap**

A structured breakdown of features for the MVP launch and the Phase 2 expansion of a platform designed for Australian social clubs, community groups, and scouting organisations.

---

# 🟢 **MVP (Minimum Viable Product)**

## **1. Group Website Builder**

- Customisable templates for clubs, scouts, sports teams, hobby groups
- Drag‑and‑drop sections (About, Join Us, Events, News, Gallery)
- Mobile‑friendly, accessible design
- Optional custom domains

---

## **2. Membership & Joining**

- Online membership forms
- Custom fields (e.g., emergency contacts, WWCC number)
- Approval workflows
- Membership tiers (adult, junior, family)
- Renewal reminders
- Digital membership cards

---

## **3. Payments & Fundraising**

- Integrated payments (Stripe, PayPal)
- Sell memberships, events, merchandise
- Donation module
- Automated receipts

---

## **4. Events & Ticketing**

- Event creation with capacity limits
- Free or paid tickets
- QR code check‑in
- Volunteer sign‑up slots

---

## **5. Merchandise Store**

- Simple e‑commerce
- Inventory tracking
- Pre‑order campaigns

---

## **6. Member Portal**

- Update personal details
- Access tickets, receipts, membership status
- Download documents

---

## **7. Communication Tools**

- Email newsletters
- In‑platform announcements
- Scheduled posts

---

## **8. Administration & Compliance**

- Role‑based permissions
- Document storage (policies, risk assessments)
- Attendance logs
- Basic WWCC tracking

---

## **9. Financial Tools**

- Payment dashboard
- Export to Xero / MYOB
- Reconciliation tools

---

# 🔵 **Phase 2 (Growth & Expansion)**

## **1. Multi‑Group / Multi‑Branch Support**

- Parent organisation dashboards
- Sub‑group sites under one umbrella
- Shared branding and templates

---

## **2. Advanced Volunteer Management**

- Volunteer profiles
- Skill tagging
- Roster creation
- Hours tracking and reporting

---

## **3. Learning & Badge Systems**

- Badge progress tracking
- Online learning modules
- Parent/guardian view

---

## **4. Enhanced Community Features**

- Public directory of groups
- “Find a group near me”
- Cross‑group collaboration tools
- Community feed (optional)

---

## **5. Photo & Media Enhancements**

- Secure galleries with granular permissions
- Event‑based tagging
- Youth‑safe sharing controls

---

## **6. Supplier Portal (B2B Marketplace)**

- Companies list products (uniforms, equipment, fundraising items)
- Special pricing for clubs
- Affiliate commissions
- Seasonal campaigns

---

## **7. Community Group Marketplace**

- Groups add supplier products to their store
- Automated commission payouts
- Bulk‑buy discounts
- Fundraising product catalogues

---

## **8. Advanced Integrations**

- Google Workspace / Microsoft 365
- Social media auto‑posting
- Calendar sync (Google, Apple, Outlook)
- API for mobile apps

---

## **9. Platform Infrastructure Enhancements**

- White‑label options for national bodies
- Audit logs
- API access for partners
- Multi‑tenant scaling improvements

---

# 🟣 **Long‑Term Vision (Phase 3+)**

_(Optional but helpful for strategic planning)_

- Mobile app for members and admins
- AI‑powered admin assistant (automated reminders, insights, compliance alerts)
- Print‑on‑demand merchandise
- National‑level reporting for large organisations
- Grants & sponsorship marketplace
